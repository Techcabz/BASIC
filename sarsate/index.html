Remedios T. Romualdez, officially the Municipality of Remedios T. Romualdez, is a 5th class municipality in the province of Agusan del Norte, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 17,155 people. The town is one of the leading rice producers of Agusan del Norte province.
